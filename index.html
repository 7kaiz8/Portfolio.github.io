Work Stages

01

INQUIRY

The client reaches out explaining what they want—project type, style, size, deadlines, and any important details.

02

AGREEMENT

You discuss scope, pricing, rights/usage, deadlines, and expectations. Both parties confirm the terms (written agreement, email confirmation, or contract).

03

DOWN PAYMENT

Often a deposit (30–50%) is taken before work begins. This ensures commitment from both sides.

04

DRAFT PHASE

You create and share a rough sketch, layout, or concept for approval. Client can request minor revisions here.

05

FINAL PAYMENT

Once the client approves the final work, they send the remaining payment. After payment, you deliver the final unwatermarked files or physical piece.

06

PROJECT DELIVERY

You complete the final piece and deliver it in the agreed format (digital files, prints, etc.). Watermarked previews may be sent until payment is complete.
